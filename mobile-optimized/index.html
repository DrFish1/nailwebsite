About Leanna

Hi! I'm Leanna, a passionate 21-year-old nail technician who discovered my love for nail artistry during college.

What started as a creative hobby quickly became my calling. I specialize in Gel-X extensions and intricate nail art that tells your unique story.

Every set I create is crafted with precision, care, and attention to detail. I believe your nails should be a reflection of your personality and style.

When I'm not creating beautiful nails, you'll find me exploring new techniques, staying up-to-date with the latest trends, and always learning to perfect my craft.
